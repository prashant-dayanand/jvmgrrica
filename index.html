ABOUT US

The JVMGRR Institute of Computer Applications was established in 1999. The institute offers three-year full time regular Masters of Computer Applications (M.C.A.) programme, to facilitate a multifaceted and multi-dimensional growth of professional education. The institute is affiliated to Maharshi Dayanand University, Rohtak for MCA programme and is duly approved by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE).
